About Us

Trimurti Stainlink Equipments Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneer in the manufacture of Stainless-Steel Enclosures since 2002. It is a premier one source ISO 9001-2015 certified company for enclosures in SS304 and SS316 material standard. Its products and services are promoted under the TruStar brand. The TruStar brand has created a niche for itself in the field of enclosure manufacturing, constantly working towards incorporating new technologies to give its customers on time delivery with the best product and service. TruStar offers a wide range of CE, ATEX, IECEx and PESO approved Enclosures and also offers manufactured custom built large panels like MCC - compartmental / non-compartmental, Modular, Desk panel, Cable Trays in SS304 and SS316 material standard. TruStar is an expert full service supplier to fulfil the needs of customers across the manufacturing spectrum for high quality enclosures. The company offers Tanks, Skids, Cable tray, to sectors with harsh and aggressive environments requiring the highest levels of hygiene like the dairy, food industry, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, oil & gas,railways and packaging industries.

Trimurti Engineering

Trimurti Engineering added stainless steel customised panel manufacturing to its portfolio.

Acquired Facilities

Acquired cutting edge CNC cutting and bending facilities.

Shifted To The Next Level

Trimurti Engineering shifted to the next level OR market for stainless steel enclosures.

2012

Trimurti Engineering converted to Trimurti Stainlink Equipments Private Limited

Trimurti Engineering converted to Trimurti Stainlink Equipments Private Limited to take up higher challenges.

2015

Trimurti Stainlink Equipments Pvt. Ltd. achieves CE certification

In its unrelenting quest for excellence, Trimurti Stainlink Equipments Pvt. Ltd. achieves CE certification for its stainless steel enclosures. It also began market expansion all over India.

2018

Trimurti Stainlink Equipments Pvt. Ltd. establishes the TruStar

Trimurti Stainlink Equipments Pvt. Ltd. establishes the TruStar brand to increase market visibility. Acquires advanced equipment and infrastructure to manufacture stainless steel enclosures under a single roof and achieved the ATEX certification in doing so. Established a new facility to manufacture tanks and custom built stainless steel products. Started a new vertical for outsourced electronic product development for its European customer.

2022

Manufacturing facilities moved

Manufacturing facilities moved to a state of the art plant spanning 100000 sq ft at village Velu off the NH4 Pune Bengaluru highway.
